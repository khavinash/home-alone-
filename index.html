About Home Alone House

The home itself was built in 1920 in the Georgian colonial style—perfectly symmetrical. According to Zillow, the home now boasts 5,398 square feet, 10 bedrooms, and 5 baths. It's Chris Columbus, the director of the film, whom we can hold responsible for finding the iconic Chicago suburb house.

The house from Home Alone has gone on sale for $5.25 million in the suburbs of Chicago.And for the opportunity to rent the house for one night only – and for just $25.
